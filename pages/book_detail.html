Title: The Giver

Author: Lois Lowry

Published: 1993

Genre: Young Adult, Dystopian

Page Count: 240

Summary:

The Giver is a 1993 American young adult dystopian novel by Lois Lowry. It is set in a society which at first appears to be utopian but is later revealed to be dystopian. The novel follows a 12-year-old boy named Jonas who is selected to be the Receiver of Memory, a prestigious position in his community. As he begins to learn about the true nature of his society, he discovers the dark secrets that lie beneath its surface.
